# Teaching at Arizona State University

I have been serving as a Teaching Assistant for the School of Computing and Augmented Intelligence at Arizona State University, supporting undergraduate courses in programming, data structures, and theoretical computer science since Fall 2023.

## Fall 2023
- **FSE404: Engineering Projects in Community Service**: Assisted students in project planning, provided guidance on community-oriented engineering challenges, and evaluated project deliverables.
- **CSE110: Introduction to Programming in Java**: Conducted weekly discussion sections, held office hours, and assisted labs to reinforce fundamental Java programming concepts.
- **CSE205: Object Orientation, Data Structures and Algorithms**: Assisted lab sessions on object-oriented design and algorithm implementation, and provided feedback on programming projects.

## Spring 2024
- **CSE205: Object Orientation, Data Structures and Algorithms (Lead TA)**: Promoted to Lead Teaching Assistant; managed creation of review materials, coordinated a team of 60 UGTAs and tutors, and developed YouTube playlist tutorials that garnered over 70,000 views.

## Fall 2024 & Spring 2025
- **CSE205: Object Orientation, Data Structures and Algorithms**: Continued support as TA, facilitated lab exercises on advanced data structures, and assisted with exam preparation workshops.
